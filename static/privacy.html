Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

FitToHire ("we", "our", "us") operates fittohire.in. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: name, email address, profession, resume, certificates, and interview session data. We also collect usage data such as session scores, badges earned, and login activity.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide the FitToHire platform services — including mock interview sessions, AI scoring, badge awards, and your public proof profile. We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

Data Storage

Your data including uploaded resumes and certificates are stored securely using Supabase. Your data is preserved even if your subscription is paused, and is only deleted upon your explicit written request to auditdesk.hq@gmail.com.

Payments

All payments are processed by Razorpay. We do not store your card or payment details. Razorpay's privacy policy governs payment data handling.

Public Profile

Your proof profile page at fittohire.in/profile/[username] is publicly visible and indexed by search engines. You can toggle your visibility off at any time from your account settings.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for login sessions and platform functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Contact

For any privacy concerns, email us at auditdesk.hq@gmail.com.
