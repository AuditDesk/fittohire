Privacy Policy
Last updated: 29 March 2026

FitToHire ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Audit Desk ([email protected]). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use fittohire.in.

By using FitToHire, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following information when you use FitToHire:

  • Email address — required to create your account and send your login link
  • Full name and location — optional, added by you in your profile setup
  • Interview responses — your text answers to mock interview questions
  • AI-generated scores — scores and feedback generated from your responses
  • Profile information — profession, availability status, bio, LinkedIn URL
  • Payment information — processed securely by Razorpay; we do not store card details
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, session timestamps

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and operate the FitToHire platform
  • To generate AI-scored interview assessments and certificates
  • To display your public proof profile to employers (only if you opt in)
  • To send you login links and transactional emails
  • To send welcome emails and platform updates
  • To process subscription payments via Razorpay
  • To improve our AI scoring models and platform features

3. Who can see your information

Your public profile — if you set your profile to public, your name, profession, location, scores and badges are visible to anyone including employers. Your email is only shown if you explicitly enable contact visibility in your profile settings.

Subscribed employers — employers with an active subscription can read your interview answers if your profile is public. You can set your profile to private at any time in your profile settings.

We do not sell your data to any third party under our current model.

4. Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS in the Asia-Pacific region). We use row-level security to ensure users can only access their own data. Passwords are never stored — we use magic link authentication only.

Payment transactions are processed by Razorpay and governed by their security standards. We do not store any card or UPI credentials.

5. Cookies and tracking

We use HTTP-only session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking scripts. We do not use Google Analytics or any similar tracking service.

6. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please email us at [email protected] and we will process your request within 7 business days.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data in your profile
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Set your profile to private at any time
  • Opt out of contact visibility so employers cannot see your email

8. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Razorpay — payment processing
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI interview question generation and scoring
  • Railway — application hosting
  • Cloudflare — domain and security

Each of these services has their own privacy policy. We share only the minimum data necessary for each service to function.

9. Children's privacy

FitToHire is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes by email. Continued use of FitToHire after changes
