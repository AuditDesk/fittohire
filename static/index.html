Why FitToHire exists

The hiring process is exhausting.
For everyone.

Before FitToHire — as a job seeker

Update your resume. Apply to 50 jobs. Hear back from 3. Take half a day off work. Travel across the city. Wait in a room with 20 others. Interview on a bad day. Wait two weeks. Get rejected with no feedback. Repeat.

Before FitToHire — as an employer

Post a job. Get 200 applications. Screen 40. Shortlist 10. Schedule 8. Three show up. One is right. They're serving a 90-day notice. Start again.

Both sides are losing time they don't have, on a process that was never designed to work this well.
