Refund Policy
Last updated: 29 March 2026

This Refund Policy applies to all payments made on FitToHire (fittohire.in). Please read it carefully before subscribing or making any purchase.

1. Job seeker subscriptions

Monthly plan — ₹199/month

You may cancel your monthly subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future billing. We do not provide refunds for the current billing period — your access continues until the end of the paid period.

Annual plan — ₹1,799/year

Annual subscriptions may be refunded within 7 days of purchase if you have not used the platform (i.e. have not completed any interview sessions). After 7 days or after completing any session, annual subscriptions are non-refundable. To request a refund, email [email protected] with your registered email and payment details.

2. Employer subscriptions

Employer subscription — ₹1,999/month

You may cancel your employer subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future billing. We do not provide refunds for the current billing period — your access continues until the end of the paid period.

3. Job post fees

Job post fees (₹499 per post) are non-refundable once your job post has been activated and made live on the platform. If a technical error on our part prevented your post from going live, please contact us within 48 hours and we will investigate and issue a refund if appropriate.

4. How to cancel your subscription

To cancel your subscription:

  • Email us at [email protected] with your registered email address
  • We will cancel your subscription and confirm by email within 1 business day
  • Your access continues until the end of your current billing period

Alternatively, you can cancel your UPI autopay mandate directly through your bank's UPI app or through Razorpay's customer portal.

5. Refund processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days and credited to your original payment method. Razorpay processing times may vary. If you have not received your refund after 10 business days, please contact us.

6. Exceptions

We may consider refunds outside this policy at our discretion in exceptional circumstances — for example, duplicate payments, technical failures on our side, or other situations where a refund is clearly warranted. Contact us and we will do our best to resolve the situation fairly.

7. Chargebacks

If you raise a chargeback with your bank before contacting us, we reserve the right to suspend your account pending investigation. We encourage you to contact us first — we are always willing to resolve disputes directly and fairly.
