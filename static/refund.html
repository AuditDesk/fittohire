Refund Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

FitToHire operates a strict no-refund policy. All subscription payments — monthly and annual — are non-refundable once processed.

Why No Refunds?

FitToHire provides immediate access to AI-powered interview sessions, scoring, and your proof profile from the moment your subscription is activated. Since the service is delivered digitally and instantly upon payment, we are unable to offer refunds.

Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Upon cancellation, your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation. Your account data, scores, badges and uploaded files will be preserved even after cancellation — you can resubscribe at any time to regain full access.

Job Posting Fees (Employers)

Job posting fees paid by employers are non-refundable. A posted job remains active for 30 days from the date of posting. There are no refunds if the job is closed before the 30-day period ends.

Technical Issues

If you experience a technical issue that prevents you from accessing the platform, please contact us at auditdesk.hq@gmail.com. We will investigate and resolve the issue promptly. In cases of verified platform unavailability exceeding 48 hours, we may at our sole discretion offer a subscription extension.
